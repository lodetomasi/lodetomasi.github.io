About me

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of L'Aquila, working on AI security and LLM-driven code protection under the supervision of the Software Engineering group. I also hold a position as Head of Core Platforms at SIAE, where I lead the engineering team responsible for platform modernization and ML/AI infrastructure. I received my MS in Applied Data Science from the University of L'Aquila, focusing on machine learning applications in industrial settings.

My research focuses on the intersection of large language models and software security, particularly developing novel approaches to code obfuscation and protection using generative AI. I am investigating the paradox of LLMs that can identify vulnerabilities with high accuracy yet generate vulnerable code, with applications to secure software engineering practices. Furthermore, I work on scalable ML systems for enterprise environments, having deployed production systems processing over 1TB of data daily.

You can find a list of my publications at Google Scholar and ORCID.
